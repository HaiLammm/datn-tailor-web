# Executive Summary

**tailor_project** is an AI-powered bespoke tailoring platform specializing in Vietnamese Áo dài. The platform serves three core functions:

1. **AI Bespoke Engine:** The **Physical-Emotional Compiler** translates abstract style adjectives into precise geometric deltas (**Geometric Delta**), ensuring physical and aesthetic fit while digitizing and preserving artisan heritage knowledge.
2. **E-commerce & Rental:** A full-featured digital storefront for purchasing and renting Áo dài, with product catalog, cart, checkout, and integrated payment processing.
3. **Operations & CRM:** Role-based dashboards for shop owner (revenue, orders, appointments, CRM), tailors (assigned tasks, income tracking), and customers (order history, booking).

**Target Users:**
- **Customer (Linh):** Browses, buys/rents Áo dài, books appointments for bespoke consultations.
- **Owner (Cô Lan):** Manages products, orders, appointments, tailors, leads, vouchers, and monitors business KPIs.
- **Tailor (Minh):** Receives assigned tasks, tracks production, reports income, and executes AI-guided cutting patterns.

**Core Problem:** The "language gap" between customer emotional desires and tailor technical execution — combined with a lack of digital infrastructure for order management, scheduling, and customer relationship management in heritage tailor shops.

**Solution:** A unified platform combining computational geometry AI with a complete e-commerce and operations backbone, enabling heritage shops to scale without losing craftsmanship quality.
